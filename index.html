About Me

I'm a new mom who decided it was time to learn a new skill that would help me find a career I love and am passionate about. After an extensive search, I landed on Software Engineering for 3 reasons:

  1. I enjoy learning new ways to creatively use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  2. Software Engineering is constantly changing, it allows me to continue to research and grow my skills with each new project.
  3. I love connecting with other developers and getting their perspective on my work!
